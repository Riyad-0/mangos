  John Mango was just seven years old when his uncle, world-famous chef Gordon Mango, showed him around the kitchen at Mango Steakhouse. Ever since then John developed an obsession with all things cooking. He spent the next several years of his life training under his uncle and traveling the world to discover what makes food truly great.
  Having inherited the restaurant from his uncle, John is committed to delivering world-class culinary experiences to each and every customer, backed by the Mango Mandate: if you do not finish your meal with a smile on your face, you are free to leave without having to pay.
