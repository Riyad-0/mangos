  Kwame Baffour was just seven years old when his uncle, world-famous chef Gordon Baffour, showed him around the kitchen at Mango Steakhouse. Ever since then Kwame developed an obsession with all things cooking. He spent the next several years of his life training under his uncle and traveling the world to discover what makes food truly great.
  Having inherited the restaurant from his uncle, Kwame is committed to delivering world-class culinary experiences to each and every customer, backed by the Baffour Bond: if you do not finish your meal with a smile on your face, you are free to leave without having to pay.
